Beef and Broccoli

An easy, 1-pan, 30-minute meal that is loaded with fresh broccoli, tender nutrition-packed beef, and the best stir fry sauce.

Beef and Broccoli is a classic Chinese-American stir-fry featuring tender slices of marinated flank steak, vibrant broccoli florets, and a savory brown sauce. The dish showcases a perfect harmony of textures and flavors, with the beef's umami richness complemented by the crispness of blanched broccoli. Infused with garlic, ginger, and a well-balanced sauce, this quick and flavorful stir-fry is commonly served over steamed white rice, embodying the delicious simplicity of Chinese-American cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 pound broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup beef broth or water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for stir-frying)
  • Cooked white rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Marinate the Beef
    • In a bowl, combine thinly sliced beef with soy sauce, cornstarch, and sesame oil. Let it marinate for at least 15-30 minutes.
  2. Blanch the Broccoli
    • Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the broccoli florets and cook for about 2 minutes, just until they turn bright green. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce
    • In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sugar, and beef broth or water. Set aside.
  4. Stir-Fry
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the marinated beef and stir-fry until it's browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the wok and set it aside.
  5. Cook the Vegetables
    • In the same wok, add another tablespoon of oil. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the blanched broccoli and stir-fry for a couple of minutes.
  6. Combine and Sauce
    • Return the cooked beef to the wok with the broccoli. Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and broccoli. Toss everything together until well-coated and heated through.
  7. Serve
    • Serve the Beef and Broccoli over cooked white rice.

Adjust the seasoning and sauce quantities according to your taste preferences. You can also add additional vegetables like bell peppers or carrots for more variety. Enjoy your homemade Beef and Broccoli!
