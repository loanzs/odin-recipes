Pan Seared Steak

Season, sear, and savor the perfect blend of crispy crust and juicy tenderness.

Pan-seared steak is a culinary delight, featuring a quality cut like ribeye or New York strip generously seasoned and brought to room temperature. In a hot, oiled pan, the meat sizzles, creating a caramelized crust while maintaining a juicy interior. The searing process, just minutes on each side, captures succulence, delivering a mouthwatering contrast of crispy exterior and tender, preferred center. It's a timeless, delectable experience, each bite releasing a burst of savory goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless ribeye or New York strip steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: Garlic cloves, fresh herbs (rosemary or thyme)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Steaks
    • Take the steaks out of the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
    • Pat the steaks dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat the Pan
    • Heat a heavy-duty skillet or cast-iron pan over medium-high heat. Add olive oil and allow it to get hot but not smoking.
  3. Sear the Steaks
    • Carefully place the steaks in the hot pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes on one side without moving them to achieve a nice crust.
    • Flip the steaks using tongs and sear the other side for an additional 3-4 minutes. Adjust the time based on your desired doneness.
  4. Add Flavor (Optional)
    • In the last minute of cooking, add butter, garlic, and fresh herbs to the pan. Tilt the pan and spoon the melted butter over the steaks.
  5. Rest and Slice
    • Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute.
    • Slice the steaks against the grain and serve.
  6. Optional: Pan Sauce
    • If desired, use the pan drippings to make a simple pan sauce. Add a splash of broth or wine to the hot pan, scrape up any browned bits, and let it reduce slightly. Pour the sauce over the sliced steaks.

Enjoy your delicious pan-seared steak! Feel free to customize the recipe with your preferred seasonings and sides.
