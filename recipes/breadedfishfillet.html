Breaded Fish Fillet

Indulge in the exquisite simplicity of breaded fish fillet, a culinary delight that brings together the crispy allure of a perfectly seasoned exterior and the tender, flaky goodness within. Immaculately coated and expertly pan-fried, each bite offers a symphony of textures and flavors that elevate the humble fish fillet to a satisfying and sophisticated dish. Whether enjoyed on its own or paired with a zesty dipping sauce, this creation promises a delightful seafood experience that's both easy to prepare and a true celebration of culinary craftsmanship.

Ingredients

  • 4 fish fillets (cod, tilapia, or your preferred white fish)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Breading Station
  2. Coat the Fish
  3. Heat the Oil
  4. Fry the Fillets
  5. Drain and Serve

Enjoy your delicious homemade breaded fish fillets!
